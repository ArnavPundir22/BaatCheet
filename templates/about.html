About BaatCheet

Ephemeral, Instant Video & Text Chat

BaatCheet is a next-generation ephemeral chat platform designed for spontaneous, secure communication. Rooms are disposable, meaning once everyone leaves, the room and its data vanish completely.

Features

  • Instant Video & Audio Communication
  • Real-time Text Chat
  • Ephemeral Rooms (Self-destructing)
  • No Registration Required
  • Glassmorphism Premium UI

Developer

Designed and developed by Arnav Pundir. Built with a focus on seamless, real-time interactions and modern web aesthetics.
